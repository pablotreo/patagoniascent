Cerro Solo 2121msnm.

Between the valleys of Cerro Torre and Rio Tunel emerges this solitary mountain, of singular stature and imposing views. From here we can see the Southern Patagonian Ice Field, the Viedma Lake, and the mountain ranges of both Cerro Torre and Fitz Roy. To conquer it we must ascend the 50° slopes of its top glacier. A great challenge for all those who want to have their first experiences on a Patagonian summit.

Difficulty: Medium.

Duration: 3 days.

Pax: 1 or 2 people.

Recommended time: October to March.
