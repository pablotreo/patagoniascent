North Patagonia - Bariloche and surroundings

This ski trip in the area of Bariloche and its surroundings will take us to different places in search of the best snow conditions and weather. Fantastic views of this region will be our reward for the effort made. Some of the possibilities in Bariloche include Cerro López, Frey, Baguales and Frontera. For those interested in a more extensive tour we offer the Tour of the Volcanoes, majestic formations, some of them are actually active whilst we are skiing them! It is another of the possibilities of North Patagonia. The itinerary can be designed and adapted according to the availability of days and the physical and technical level of the group.

Difficulty: Low to High.

Duration: 3 to 7 days.

Pax: 1 to 4 people.

Recommended time: July to September.
