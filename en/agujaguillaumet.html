Aguja Guillaumet 2579msnm

This satellite needle of the Fitz Roy range is often one of the first serious objectives to be carried out in Patagonia. A great adventure for those who want to climb these challenging mountains. It has different routes depending on the time of year, as well as the interest of the climber. Forests, moraine and glaciers are some of the terrains we need to tackle to reach this summit.

Difficulty: High.

Duration: 3 days.

Pax: 1 or 2 people.

Recommended time: November to March.
