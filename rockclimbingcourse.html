Rock Climbing Course Level I and II

The objective of the courses are to immerse the student in the world of rock climbing. They are designed for those people who are beginning and also for those who have previous knowledge in this discipline. We will form and build a solid foundation with regard to safety knowledge in rock climbing and develop climbers that can continue on with the desired skills, fluency and autonomy in many different aspects of sport climbing. We will also help achieve the technical objectives of the people who practice this sport.

Course duration:

4 theoretical classes

5 full-day outdoor classes on rock.

1 day of theoretical and practical exam.
