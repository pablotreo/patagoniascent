South Patagonia – El Chalten

El Chaltén is one of the best and least explored areas of ski mountaineering in Argentina with a long winter season and magnificent snow. The great variety of places, orientations and inclinations it is possible to ski even in the Patagonian climate. Skiing in the midst of the great mountains, such as Fitz Roy and Cerro Torre, make it unique. From one to ten days of skiing in the best Patagonian snow near the small town of El Chalten is a great mission to add to your adventure. Cerro Vespignani, Mosquito, Crestón and Madsen are some of the places we ski in South Patagonia. The program is flexible in relation to the number of days and the itinerary can be adapted according to your schedule and physical condition.

Difficulty: Low to High.

Duration: 1 to 10 days.

Pax: 1 or 2 people.

Recommended time: July to September.
