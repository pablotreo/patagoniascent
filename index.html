We are three friends Mountain Guides IFMGA / UIAGM. We live in El Chalten most of the year where we practice our profession and develop our passion for Climbing and Skiing. Over the years we have gained a lot of experience in this region, which we would like to pass on by opening the doors to all those who want to live the Patagonian Experience, in a Safe and unforgettable way.
